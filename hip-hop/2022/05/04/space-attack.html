Space Attack

May 4, 2022

In 1996 I was crazy enough to release a instrumental hip hop vinyl LP called Space Attack featuring the hottest German hip hop producers of that time on my label Braindamage Records.


I made 660 copies of it and still today you can find them online, for example here.

The cover is from Perry Rhodan cover artist Alfred Kelsner, the graffiti on the back from Can Two.

Here is the track list:

  • Doze - Space Attack
  • Klammericious - Kungfusion
  • MoE’st Wanted Prod. & Jelly Jam - Miles Away
  • Ko Lute - That’s The Way It Should Be Done (‘92 Raw Mix)
  • Klammericious, Outsider & Doze - Cut’s & Effects
  • Cooley Blast - Coolin’ Out
  • Outsider - Outer Limits
  • Liquid Style Session - Phuture Phunk Decompression Kit
  • Scope - Cologne Costranostra!
